MediConnect Disease prediction tools

What is disease predictor?

We at MediConnect have trained machine learning model for variour diseases. These models have been trained on millions of real world data. These can be used for predicting whether you have some disease or not based on the values of your lab test reports.

Please note that the prediction are made by computer and not by expert doctor. It is very important to understand the model may not be correct everytime. Please do not panic if your test results are positive. Consult a doctor before coming to any conclusion.
Do not take any kind of medication without consulting any practiced doctor.


How to use our models:

Using our model are prety easy. Here is how you can use it:
1. Scroll to the bottom of this page and select appropriate model (select one related to disease you want to check for).
2. That will redirect you to predictor. There input all the values asked from you lab reports and click submit.
3. Once you click submit, the result will appear on the bottom of that page.

Do not take any kind of medication without consulting any practiced doctor.


General notes:

All the fields in the form page are required in order to make accurate prediction.
